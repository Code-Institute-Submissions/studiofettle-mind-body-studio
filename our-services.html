Proprietor Bio

Rachael, owner of Studiofettle, prides herself in promoting health and wellbeing. With a career stemming from customer service, mental health and personal training, the studio is a haven for feel good services.

Rachael herself delivers PT sessions for all levels tailoring every session to your needs. With a portfolio of clients, including injury, pre wedding, pre and post baby, there are success stories galore.

"I PT various clients, all with a story to tell. So we tailor their sessions to their individual requirements. The main thing is together we reach their goals, build their confidence and they leave feeling fantastic".

Studiofettle is not a gym its a wellbeing center that promotes healthy body, healthy mind and a positive you!
